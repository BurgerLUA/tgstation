18 March 2021

ATHATH updated:

  • Fixes the goof(s) made in another coder's anti-spider plasmaflooding PR that rendered silicons unable to interact with many machines.
  • Admins with "ai interact" mode on can now interact with any machine that is powered and not broken, regardless of other conditions, provided that the default machine can_interact() proc hasn't been overwritten in a weird way for that machine.
  • The structure of the default state of the can_interact() proc for machines has been reworked to be less awkward and painful to read than it was before.

Arkatos updated:

  • Fixed juggernaut transformation option doing nothing.
  • Fixed artificer transformation option doing nothing.
  • Fixed adult slime transformation option doing nothing.

Fikou updated:

  • fixes the ancient bundle not working at all

Maurukas updated:

  • IceBox's mining mech bay has moved to the east of the cargo warehouse
  • A number of anomalous floating decals and air alarms inside doors have been in the Icebox mining base

Sylphet updated:

  • changed berry ice cream sandwich and cornuto to use ice cream cone for consistency
  • fixed broken ice cream recipes

TheBonded updated:

  • Jade lanterns can now be found in the black market uplink
  • Added the chainsaw and switchblade to the list of items available in the black market uplink.

Timberpoes updated:

  • Fixes oversight in DNA Consoles where doing a transfer from the Genetic Makeup tab had no cooldown and could be spammed to massively irradiate things.

TribesChen updated:

  • Rocket boots, a bad idea from the black market that hurls you 20 tiles in the direction you're facing. Beware of walls and people.

necromanceranne updated:

  • Removes 9x19mm as an ammo type.
  • PP-95's (the surplus gun for nukies) now use 9mm. They also have a .35 damage multiplier, resulting in standard 9mm doing only 10.5 (9x19mm did 10, so not really much of a difference).
  • Properly aligns the xenobio bloodgun with the arm when facing to the side.

sergeirocks100 updated:

  • Malt liquor is now available in the booze dispenser.
  • Reduces the price of malt liquor in the Booze-O-Mat to give some reason to buy it over other equally priced drinks that are stronger.

tralezazelart updated:

  • Orion code maybe less shit?
  • Orion Events use datums whew
